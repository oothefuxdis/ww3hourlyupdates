About WW3 Hourly Updates

WW3 Hourly Updates is an independent global escalation tracker designed to provide rapid, live-synced war updates and DEFCON-level alerts to anyone monitoring potential global conflict scenarios.

It’s built for tactical readiness, off-grid operation, and public intelligence — combining trusted feeds, manual operator control, and live visual systems into one clear interface.

Key Features

  • 🔴 Live DEFCON status remotely editable via Google Sheets
  • 📰 Curated headlines and direct manual alerts
  • 🌐 Pulls from Al Jazeera, DW, and live conflict feeds
  • 🗂️ Archives auto-save locally, even offline
