❌ Top 5 Survival Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ignoring water needs – always plan for more than you think.
  • Overpacking or underpacking – focus on essentials, not luxuries.
  • Forgetting to test gear – try tools and equipment before an emergency.
  • Lack of physical readiness – fitness matters when bugging out.
  • No communication plan – make sure others know your location and plans.

⚡ Quick Tips to Remember

  • 3 minutes without air, 3 days without water, 3 weeks without food — prioritize accordingly.
  • Use cotton balls and petroleum jelly as cheap, powerful firestarters.
  • Always have a whistle – it carries farther than yelling.
  • Layer clothing for insulation, not bulk.
  • Stay calm — panic is your worst enemy in a crisis.
