🏙️ Underground Cities & Shelters

Global Underground Infrastructure

  • 🇷🇺 Russia – Metro-2: Secret tunnels beneath Moscow believed to be for government escape.
  • 🇺🇸 USA – Cheyenne Mountain: Cold War-era NORAD complex built into a mountain.
  • 🇨🇭 Switzerland – Civil Defense Bunkers: Every citizen has bunker space by law.
  • 🇰🇵 North Korea – Deep Tunnels: Unknown depth networks built beneath Pyongyang.

Types of Underground Shelters

  • 🚇 Metro Systems: Can serve dual use for civilian evacuation and blast protection.
  • 🛡️ Military Bunkers: Hardened locations for command, storage, and defense.
  • 💰 Private Bunkers: Custom shelters built by wealthy individuals or communities.
  • 🔨 DIY Bunkers: Backyard or rural preppers using shipping containers, concrete, or earth berms.

Building or Finding Shelter

  • 📏 Depth needed to survive blast/EMP varies by weapon size and soil type.
  • 🔍 Look for public shelter registries or metro access tunnels in cities.
  • 🏗️ DIY costs can range from $5k to $50k+ depending on size/material.
  • 📚 Learn the basics of air filtration, water storage, and shielding.

Myths vs Reality

  • ✅ Some governments do have deep continuity-of-government shelters.
  • ❌ No, there are not habitable "underground cities" for every civilian.
  • ✅ Subway tunnels may be survivable but not ideal for long-term use without prep.
